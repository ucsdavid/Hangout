How we Developed

A little insight on our little project from COGS187A

What is Hangout?

Hangout is a web application designed by a team of 6 members. We designed this application in hopes of allowing the users to be able to find, and create various events. This application was made so that if you were bored you could go to this application, punch in your location, and pull up a list of current events happening right now or events happening within the coming days. The application itself is targeted towards college students and campuses ideally as not all college students will have cars, especially if you're an incoming freshman. It helps them find and make new friends as well as enjoy their hobbies with others in the areas around them. In the end it's all about having fun in good company and building long lasting friendships!
