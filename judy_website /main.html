Hello!
My name is Judy Samatmanakit

My three life passions revolve around eating, exploring, and experiencing. These three interests give me desire to travel and discover the world by either walking the land or diving the sea. One place I have always wanted to step foot on is the world's most dangerous hiking trail, Mt. Hua Shan. I normally don't like dangerous climbing, but I long for a visit to the teahouse at the end of the trail to drink that best cup of tea. A simple desire such as a taste from that cup of tea triggers my ambition to immerse the world through a newfound experience.
Aside from my personal life...
I am a forth year Cognitive Science Human-Computer Interaction student at the University of California, San Diego. I became interested in HCI when I realized that our life is a fast and non-stopping interaction with technology. Being educated in this area, I wish to improve the quality of these interactions by using human-centered design. Those designs do not derive from the aesthetic appeal but instead originate from human needs. My goal is to immerse myself in the user's world in order to gather rich data to formulate an effective design. My studies have changed the way I view our world and I believe my vision can change yours as well. I pursued my passion for User Experience Research and Design to design for life.
